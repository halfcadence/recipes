## Steps

1. Steep tea in water.

## Analysis

<div class="analysis" markdown="1">

Brew ratio: **1:53**

| Component | Amount | Ratio |
|---|---|---|
| Tea (dry leaves) | 15g | 1 |
| Cold water | 800g | 53 |
| Steep | 8–12 h cold | — |
| Yield | ~780g | — |

**A very lean 1:53 — cold water extracts slowly, so it needs far more time, not more leaf.** Over 8–12 hours the low temperature pulls sweetness and body while leaving most of the astringent tannins behind, giving a smooth, low-bitterness brew. Leaf retention is tiny against 800g of water, so nearly all of it reaches the glass.
