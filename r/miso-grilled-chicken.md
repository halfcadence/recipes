## Ingredients

| | |
|---|---|
| 200g | chicken thighs |
| 1 | white spring onion |
| 10 | sweet green peppers |
| 60g | mirin |
| 20g | Japanese sake |
| 30g | dark soy sauce |
| 40g | light-brown miso |
| | Potato starch, as needed |

## Steps

1. Combine mirin, sake, dark soy sauce, and miso to make the sauce.

2. Cut chicken thighs into portions. Dust lightly with potato starch.

3. Grill or pan-fry chicken until cooked through and nicely browned.

4. Cut spring onion into sections and grill or char alongside sweet green peppers.

5. Glaze chicken with miso sauce during the last minute of cooking.

6. Serve chicken with grilled onion and peppers.

<div class="notes" markdown="1">
## Notes

- Potato starch helps the sauce adhere and gives a slight crispness.
- Watch the original video for Chef Matsuo's technique.
