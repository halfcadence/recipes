---
title: Bay Leaf Panna Cotta
number: 4
---

## Ingredients

To bloom gelatin:

| | |
|---|---|
| 50g | milk |
| 5g | gelatin |

Everything else:

| | |
|---|---|
| 150g | milk |
| 150g | heavy cream |
| 50g | sugar |
| 3 | bay leaves |

## Steps

1. Bloom the gelatin in 50g milk.
2. Heat remaining milk, heavy cream, sugar and bay leaves until steaming (about 160°F). Do not boil.
3. Pass it through a sieve.
4. Pour into ramekins.
5. Let it set in the fridge for at least 4~5 hrs.
