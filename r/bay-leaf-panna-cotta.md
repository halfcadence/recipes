## Ingredients

To boom gelatin:
- 50g milk
- 5g gelatin

Everything else:
- 150g milk
- 150g heavy cream
- 50g sugar
- 3 bay leaves
=
## Steps

1. Bloom the gelatin in 50g milk.
1. Heat remaining milk, heavy cream, sugar and bay leaves to 160Â°C.
1. Pass it through a sieve.
1. Pour into ramekins.
1. Let it set in the fridge for at least 4~5 hrs.
