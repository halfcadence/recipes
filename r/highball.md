## Ingredients

| | |
|---|---|
| 2oz | Japanese whisky |
| | Soda water, well chilled |
| | Ice |
| | Lemon twist (optional) |

## Steps

1. Chill a highball glass in the freezer for at least 10 minutes.

2. Fill the glass with ice. Pour in whisky.

3. Top with cold soda water. Stir gently — once, vertically — to combine without killing the carbonation.

4. Garnish with a lemon twist if using.

<div class="notes" markdown="1">
## Notes

- The ratio is roughly 1:3 or 1:4 whisky to soda. Adjust to taste.
- Everything must be cold. Glass, whisky, soda. This is the entire technique.
- Stir once. Over-stirring flattens the drink.
- Suntory Toki and Nikka Days are the standard choices — light, clean, built for mixing.
</div>
